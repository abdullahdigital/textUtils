Features of TextUtils

Remove punctuation marks from your text with the click of a button. Useful for cleaning up text for analysis or readability.

Convert your text to uppercase with ease. Ideal for making text stand out or ensuring consistency.

Remove unnecessary new line characters from your text. Makes text easier to read or process.

Eliminate extra spaces between words or sentences in your text. Ensures a clean and consistent appearance.

Count the number of characters in your text. Useful for compliance checks or length restrictions.

Calculate the number of words in your text. Helpful for analyzing content or creating summaries.

Determine the number of sentences in your text. Useful for analyzing text structure or readability.

Convert your text to title case. Ideal for creating headings or titles with proper capitalization.

Reverse the order of characters in your text. Useful for encoding or creative formatting.

Check if your text reads the same forwards and backwards. Fun for language games or puzzles.

Encrypt your text using a simple cipher. Useful for secure messaging or basic privacy needs.

Decrypt previously encrypted text. Allows you to recover original messages or content.

Create a concise summary of your text. Great for condensing long articles or documents.

Search for specific text and replace it with another. Handy for editing or formatting large documents.

Compare two texts side-by-side to identify differences or similarities. Useful for content analysis or plagiarism detection.

Convert text to different cases (e.g., uppercase, lowercase, title case). Enhances text formatting and readability.
